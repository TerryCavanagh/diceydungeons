Dicey Dungeons Irish Translation Testing

Thank you so much again for offering to test the upcoming Irish translation for Dicey Dungeons! This page will tell you everything you need to know about how to access the beta.

Short version: You need to activate the game on steam with your steam code, and opt-in to the "gaeilgeoir" branch. Long version:

You first need to install Steam, if you don't already have an account. Steam is the online store that Dicey Dungeons is sold on. You can download steam from here: [Download Steam]

Next, you need to activate Dicey Dungeons on steam!

  1. Launch Steam on your computer and log into your account.
  2. Click the Games Menu.
  3. Choose Activate a Product on Steam...
  4. Follow the onscreen instructions. You'll need the steam code I sent in the email!

Finally, once the game is installed, you'll need to opt-in to the Irish Language beta. To do this, first click "manage" on the game here:

Then, properties:

Then, on the beta tab, select gaeilgeoir.

Then you're done! Enjoy!
